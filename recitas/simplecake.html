Recipe: Simple Cake

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sugar
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil

Preparation

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Grease and flour a cake pan.
  3. In a mixing bowl, combine sugar, flour, milk, eggs, baking powder, vanilla extract, and oil.
  4. Beat the mixture until smooth and pour it into the prepared pan.
  5. Bake for 30-40 minutes or until the cake is golden brown and a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  6. Remove from the oven, let cool slightly, and serve.
