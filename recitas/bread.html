Simple Homemade Bread

Ingredients

  • 1 kg of wheat flour
  • 30 g of sugar
  • 20 g of salt
  • 1 packet of dry yeast (10 g)
  • 300 mL of warm water
  • 60 mL of oil or melted butter
  • 1 egg (optional, for brushing on top)

Preparation

  • In a large bowl, mix the dry ingredients: flour, sugar, salt, and yeast.
  • Gradually add the warm water, mixing until a dough forms.
  • Add the oil or melted butter and knead the dough until smooth and elastic.
  • Let the dough rest for 1 hour, covered with a cloth, until it doubles in size.
  • Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  • Shape the dough into a loaf and place it on a greased baking tray.
  • Let it rest for another 30 minutes and brush with egg (if desired).
  • Bake in the preheated oven for about 30-40 minutes or until golden brown.
