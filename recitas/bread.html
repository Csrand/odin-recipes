Recipe: Simple Homemade Bread

Ingredients

  • 1 kg of wheat flour
  • 30 g of sugar
  • 20 g of salt
  • 10 g of dry yeast
  • 300 mL of warm water
  • 60 mL of oil or melted butter
  • 1 egg (optional, for brushing)

Preparation

  1. Mix the dry ingredients in a large bowl.
  2. Gradually add the water and oil, kneading to form a smooth dough.
  3. Let the dough rise for 1 hour in a warm place.
  4. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  5. Shape the dough into a loaf, rest for 30 minutes, and bake for 30-40 minutes.
